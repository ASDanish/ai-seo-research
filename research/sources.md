# Sources — AI-Powered SEO Content Production

A curated list of 10 practitioners working at the intersection of **AI and SEO content production**. These were chosen for evidence of *practicing* what they teach — published case studies, original research, shipped tools, or documented results — not for follower counts or search-result visibility.

**Selection criteria:**
1. **Practitioner, not commentator** — runs real campaigns, builds tools, or publishes original experiments.
2. **AI-relevant** — actively works on AI-assisted content production, generative-engine optimization (GEO/AEO), or programmatic content at scale.
3. **Recent & active** — posting/publishing in 2025–2026.
4. **High signal** — favored over generic "SEO influencer" listicle names.

Roster composition (as planned): ~5 operators with visible results, ~4 technical/tool builders, ~1 standout educator.

## Notes on who was *excluded* (and why)

To make the "high signal, not Google results" bar explicit, these were deliberately left off:
- **Generic "YouTube automation" / faceless-channel creators** — high volume, low signal; mostly affiliate funnels, not SEO practice.
- **Jason Lemkin, Pascal Bornet, large AI generalists** — huge followings but not SEO-content practitioners.
- **Brian Dean (Backlinko)** — foundational, but largely exited/sold and posts infrequently now.
- **Lily Ray** — excellent, but leans algorithm-update *analysis* more than AI content *production* specifically. (Strong alternate if a swap is ever needed.)
